About

Hackathon Event, featuring the competitions Through the Lens, Story Fam, and Inquizito as well as national level innovation hackathon. Innovation Fest is an Annual Global Innovation Festival organised by Gravity, Technical Society at Indian Institute of Information Technology Allahabad. The national level innovation hackathon known as Innovate Iterate Interrupt (III) 4.0 will also take place in which many ideation stages and some demo ready stage projects will be presented. We expect a huge participation in this hackathon because of its projects which are based on Web3, AI & ML , Private AI and Metaverse and secondly the Prizes.

FAQs

Who is eligible to participate in III 4.0?
We extend a warm welcome to students from all colleges, degree courses, branches, and CGPA backgrounds to showcase their raw talent. Additionally, female or non-binary student developers are highly encouraged to join us at III 4.0!

Where will the hackathon take place?
III 4.0 Hackathon will be held at the CC3 Building at IIIT-Allahabad.
Is there a participation fee for III 4.0?
No, there is no cost associated with participating in III 4.0. It's a community event hosted by Gravity, open to high school students, undergraduates, graduates, and working professionals alike.

Does my idea have to be related to AI/ML?
While we're enthusiastic about AI/ML, ideas from any field or domain are welcome for III 4.0. Your idea can be industry-agnostic.
How many members can apply together as a team?
Teams can consist of 2 to 4 members. You can either join an existing team or create a new one once you've started an individual application.

Can I attend the talks without participating in the hackathon?
Absolutely! The conference is open to everyone. We'll provide updates on the schedule and links to the talks as they occur.
What if I don't have a team or an idea?
Not to worry! Many participants find themselves in the same situation. We'll have team formation and ideation events to help you connect with others and brainstorm ideas together.
What if I don't want to present my hack?
Even if you feel your project is unfinished or unimpressive, we encourage you to present it anyway! Presenting your hack provides an opportunity to celebrate your accomplishments and gain valuable feedback for future improvements.
