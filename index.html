About

Hackathon Event, featuring the competitions Through the Lens, Story Fam, and Inquizito as well as national level innovation hackathon. Innovation Fest is an Annual Global Innovation Festival organised by Gravity, Technical Society at Indian Institute of Information Technology Allahabad. The national level innovation hackathon known as Innovate Iterate Interrupt (III) 4.0 will also take place in which many ideation stages and some demo ready stage projects will be presented. We expect a huge participation in this hackathon because of its projects which are based on Web3, AI & ML , Private AI and Metaverse and secondly the Prizes.

FAQs

Need clarification on something we haven’t covered?

You can write to us at hey@hackout.io too.

+Who can participate?

We welcome students from all colleges, degree courses, branches, and CGPA backgrounds to showcase their raw talent. If you're a female or non-binary student developer, we'd love to have you at Hackout!

+How much does participating cost?

Nothing! Hackout is a community event hosted by Headout. Whether you're in high school, an undergrad, a graduate or even a working professional, we'd love to have you be a part of the event. No charge!

+How many of us can apply together as a team?

We think you’re stronger as a team than you are apart. You can join or create a team with 2 to 4 members once you've started an individual application.

+What if I don't have a team or an idea?

Don't worry, most people don’t! We’ll have team formation and ideation events to help you find people to work with. Feel free to go around, tell folks you’re looking for a team, and ask if you can help out.

+Does my idea have to be related to the travel industry?

While we're passionate about travel, we welcome ideas from any industry or domain for Hackout. Your idea can be industry-agnostic.

+Where is the hackathon venue?

You can to participate in Hackout either virtually, or from one of our campus partners. If you participate offline and are not a student at one of the campuses listed below, you may need to make your own travel arrangements. 1. IIT Kharagpur 2. IIT Madras 3. IIIT Allahabad 4. Delhi Technological University 5. Online

+What if I don't want to present my hack?

Unfinished or unimpressive (so you think) projects should be presented anyway! Presenting your hack gives you a chance to be proud of what you’ve done, and a constructive perspective of where you should be headed next.

+Can I attend the talks without participating?

Yes! The conference is open to everyone. We'll keep you updated with the schedule and the links to the talks as they happen.
